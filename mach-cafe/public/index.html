Our Story

More Than Coffee —
It's a Ritual

Born from a love of the craft, Mach cafe is a space where exceptional coffee meets thoughtful hospitality. We source our beans directly from small-batch farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra — then roast them fresh every morning right here in our open-view roastery.

🌍
Direct Trade Sourcing
We partner directly with farmers to ensure fair wages and the finest quality single-origin beans year-round.
🔥
In-House Roasting
Roasted fresh every morning in our open-view roastery for peak flavour and absolute freshness.
🌿
Sustainably Operated
100% compostable packaging, zero-waste kitchen, solar-powered, and carbon-offset delivery.
🏆
Award-Winning Coffee
Best Specialty Coffee in Andhra Pradesh, 2025 — South India Barista Guild.
Our Journey

Five Years of
Crafting Perfection

From a tiny 8-seater kiosk to Vijayawada's most beloved coffee destination — our story is one of passion, patience, and really good espresso.

2021
The First Pour
Initiated work on the kiosk model implementation in Guntupalli.
2023
Cafe Setup
Successfully initiated the setup of a cafe in Gollapudi.
2024
Menu Expansion
Expanded the menu offerings by incorporating a variety of Chinese cuisine items to enhance customer choice.
2025
Branch Establishment
Contributed to the new branch setup and operational activities in Chennai activities to support business expansion.
🏆
Best Specialty Coffee — South India 2025
Awarded by the South India Barista Guild, judged across 200+ cafés statewide on bean quality, barista craft, and guest experience.
🌱
Farm to Cup
Every bean traced to its origin farm. We visit our partner farms twice a year.
🎓
Barista School
All our baristas are certified Q-graders trained in-house over 6 months.
