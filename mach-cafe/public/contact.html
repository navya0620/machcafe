Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need a reservation to visit? ›
Walk-ins are always welcome! However, for weekends, Vinyl Night (Thursdays), and groups of 4+, we strongly recommend reserving a table in advance to guarantee your preferred seating.
Is there a minimum spend for the private pods? ›
Private meeting pods have a minimum spend of ₹500 per person for bookings up to 2 hours. For longer bookings or full-day use, please contact events@mach.in for custom pricing.
Do you do private events or café buyouts? ›
Yes! We offer full and partial café buyouts for birthdays, corporate events, product launches, and more. Email events@mach.in or call us to discuss packages starting at ₹25,000.
Can I bring my pet? ›
Absolutely — pets are welcome on our rooftop garden terrace! We have a small pet menu with water bowls and dog-friendly treats. Leashes are required inside the café premises.
Do you offer home delivery or takeaway? ›
Takeaway is available for all menu items. We partner with Swiggy and Zomato for delivery within 5km. For large orders or office catering, call us directly for special packaging and pricing.
How do I sign up for Barista Masterclasses? ›
Masterclasses run every Saturday at 9 AM. Slots are limited to 12 participants. Book via WhatsApp (+91 98765 43210), Instagram DM, or in-person at the counter. Cost is ₹1,200 per person including coffee and materials.
